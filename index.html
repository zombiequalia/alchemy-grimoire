The Apothecary's Ledger

Keizaal · Master Alchemist
Ingredient Qty Source

Tap a row to expand prices (buy / labor / sell), unit weight, notes, and what it feeds. Tap the ★ to flag priority-to-forage; tap the quantity or +/− to edit. Source is a free text label. Sort by Weight ↓ to find what's bloating your carry weight. Everything saves automatically.

Potion Stock Sell

Tap a potion to expand its effect, ingredients (with what you have), cost breakdown, profit, and an editable note. Make = batches you can craft now. Sell = your player price (tap to edit). Cost & profit pull from ingredient buy/labor prices. Search matches names and ingredients.

Potion Order Stock Make

Build an order — add potions and quantities. Potions you have in stock are pulled from your shelf (no ingredients needed); only the shortfall to make pulls ingredients and gold cost. The summary tallies total ingredients to forage/buy, gold cost to brew, and the full sale value so you can quote a price.

Item Qty Location / Person

Track anything you stash anywhere — ingredients, finished potions, gear, loot — and where it is or who holds it. Free-form: type any item and location. All fields editable; saves automatically.

Backup & restore

Your ingredients, potions, and storage are saved automatically in this browser. Export a backup file to keep your data safe or move it to another device.

Install as an app

Add this to your phone's home screen for a clean app icon that opens fullscreen — no browser bars.

iPhone Open in Safari → tap the Share button → Add to Home Screen.
Android Open in Chrome → tap the ⋮ menu → Add to Home screen / Install app.

Tip: export a backup first. Phone browsers occasionally clear stored data, and a backup file restores everything in one tap.

Reset

Restore the starting data. This wipes your current ingredients, potions, and storage entries.
